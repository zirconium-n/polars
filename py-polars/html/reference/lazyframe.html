LazyFrame

Aggregation

LazyFrame.std()

Aggregate the columns in the DataFrame to their standard deviation value.

LazyFrame.var()

Aggregate the columns in the DataFrame to their variance value.

LazyFrame.max()

Aggregate the columns in the DataFrame to their maximum value.

LazyFrame.min()

Aggregate the columns in the DataFrame to their minimum value.

LazyFrame.sum()

Aggregate the columns in the DataFrame to their sum value.

LazyFrame.mean()

Aggregate the columns in the DataFrame to their mean value.

LazyFrame.median()

Aggregate the columns in the DataFrame to their median value.

LazyFrame.quantile(quantile)

Aggregate the columns in the DataFrame to their quantile value.

Descriptive stats

LazyFrame.describe_plan()

A string representation of the unoptimized query plan.

LazyFrame.describe_optimized_plan([])

A string representation of the optimized query plan.

LazyFrame.show_graph([optimized,show,])

Show a plot of the query plan.

Manipulation/ selection

LazyFrame.filter(predicate)

Filter the rows in the DataFrame based on a predicate expression.

LazyFrame.select(exprs)

Select columns from this DataFrame.

LazyFrame.groupby(by)

Start a groupby operation.

LazyFrame.join(ldf[,left_on,right_on,on,])

Add a join operation to the Logical Plan.

LazyFrame.with_columns(exprs)

Add or overwrite multiple columns in a DataFrame.

LazyFrame.with_column(expr)

Add or overwrite column in a DataFrame.

LazyFrame.drop_columns(columns)

Remove multiple columns from a DataFrame.

LazyFrame.drop_column(column)

Remove a column from the DataFrame.

LazyFrame.with_column_renamed(existing_name,)

Rename a column in the DataFrame

LazyFrame.reverse()
